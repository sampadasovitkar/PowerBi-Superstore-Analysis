//Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore dataset to analyze sales, profit, and performance across different regions, categories, and products. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

//Objective

To analyse Superstore sales data and identify:

//Key revenue and profit drivers
Regional and category-wise performance
Sales trends over time
Top-performing products
//Dataset
Platform: Kaggle
Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset
Dataset Author: Vivek (Kaggle username: vivek468)
Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final
Time period: 2014–2017
Key KPIs
Total Sales: 214.28K
Total Profit: 51.05K
Product-level performance insights
Regional contribution to sales
Dashboard Features
1. Executive Summary
KPI cards for Sales and Profit
High-level performance overview
2. Sales Trend Analysis
Year-wise sales trend visualization
Identification of growth and decline patterns
3. Regional Analysis
Sales distribution across regions
Identification of top-performing regions
4. Category & Sub-Category Insights
Performance comparison across categories
Drill-down into sub-category contributions
5. Product Performance
Top-selling products ranked by revenue
Identification of high-impact items
6. Interactive Filters
Region filter
Category filter
Order date range slicer

//Key Insights
Sales show an overall upward trend with fluctuations in earlier years
West region contributes the highest share of sales
A small number of products generate a significant portion of revenue
Office Supplies category shows strong volume but moderate profitability

//Business Recommendations
Focus on high-margin product categories to improve profitability
Strengthen sales strategy in underperforming regions
Optimise discount strategy to protect profit margins
Prioritise inventory planning for top-performing products

//Tools Used
Power BI Desktop
Power Query (Data Cleaning)
Data Visualisation Techniques

//Outcome

This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand business performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed strategic decisions based on data.
